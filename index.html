about our agency

MUSE is a modelling and talent agency centered around the world and with a sole mission to promote modelling, talents and confidence in all parts of Africa; thereby introducing the black culture to the masses. We are a firm believer that beauty goes beyond colour and we see the elegant and radiant beauty in blacks. We are hereby pushing for the next big thing for all our African Muse; so prepare for the revolution and in the mean time; celebrate the beauty...
